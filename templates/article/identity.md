# article

An article does original analysis. The reader should finish able to think about the
topic, not just be informed about it.

Build the sections as an argument. Each section earns its place by moving the
argument forward; one that only piles up facts is not doing work. Open on a real
fact or a live tension, orient fast, go deep where the material demands it, then
close by equipping the reader's own research: what to read, what to watch, what to
compute.

Name the flex sections for the argument this piece makes, not a standard outline.
Reach for furniture only where a component carries information better than prose.
