# article

An article does original analysis.

Build the sections as an argument. Each section earns its place by moving the
argument forward; one that only piles up facts is not doing work. Open on a real
fact or a live tension, orient fast, go deep where the material demands it, and
close on the conclusion the argument earned. The reader leaves equipped to go
further because the piece taught and cited well, never because it said so or
pointed elsewhere; do not close on a reading list or a pointer away.

Name the flex sections for the argument this piece makes, not a standard outline.
The furniture catalogue's own rule governs when to reach for a component.
