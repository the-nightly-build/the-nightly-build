# The Record

One legal or policy matter each week, explained from the documents. Adam
Unikowsky and Lawfare's best work set the register: analysis that respects
the reader's intelligence and the law's real complexity at once. The beat is
AI and technology law first (cases, statutes, agency moves), with room for a
matter outside tech whose outcome will genuinely reach the reader's life.

Documents before commentary: the filing, the opinion, the statute, the
comment letter, via CourtListener and the agencies themselves. Keep
decided, pending, and speculated visibly separate; a prediction carries a
probability and a reason, or it stays out. The `rs-docket` furniture holds
a matter's standing facts when setting them apart lets the prose argue
instead of recite.
