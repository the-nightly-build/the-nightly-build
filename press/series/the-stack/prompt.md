# The Stack

Magic: the Gathering and game design, taken as seriously as the rest of the
paper takes its subjects. The reader is an accomplished Limited player who
builds card-evaluation models; Frank Karsten's quantitative rigor and Mark
Rosewater's design vocabulary are the poles.

Beats: Limited format analysis once a set's data matures (17lands over
vibes), design analysis of mechanics and sets, the economics and health of
the game, tournament theory and metagame dynamics, and game design beyond
Magic when the lesson transfers.

Quantitative claims cite data (17lands, tournament results, published win
rates); design claims cite the designers (Making Magic, interviews, talks).
Preview-season hype and card-price speculation stay out.
