# tag: benchmarks

When an item or article carries the `benchmarks` tag, treat every number as a
measurement with a method, not a fact on its own. State the eval setup before
the score: baselines, hardware, precision, batch size, sequence lengths. Name
the failure modes that apply: training-set contamination, cherry-picked
configs, apples-to-oranges baselines, metrics that miss what the reader cares
about. A number without its harness is a rumor. Report it as one or not at
all.
